Celsius to Farenheit Conversion

The formula to convert Celsius to Farenheit is:
F=(C x 9/5) +32

Farenheit to Celsius Conversion

The formula to convert Farenheit to Celsius is:
C=(F-32) x 5/9

Kilograms to Pounds Conversion

The formula to convert Kilogram to Pounds is:
lbs=kg x 2.20462

Pounds to Kilograms Conversion

The formula to convert Pounds to Kilograms is:
kg=lbs+2.20462
